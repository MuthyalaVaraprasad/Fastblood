🩸 FastBlood – Emergency Blood Donation & Donor Management Platform

FastBlood is a smart healthcare web application designed to connect blood donors, recipients, hospitals, and blood banks through a centralized digital platform. The system helps users quickly find blood donors during emergencies, reducing the time required to locate life-saving blood units.

The platform aims to bridge the gap between blood donors and patients by providing an accessible, efficient, and responsive solution for blood donation management.

🌐 Project Overview

Every second matters during a medical emergency. Finding compatible blood donors at the right time can save lives.

FastBlood was developed to simplify the process of locating blood donors, managing donor information, and improving communication between donors and recipients through a user-friendly digital platform.

The project focuses on creating a reliable healthcare solution that promotes voluntary blood donation and supports emergency medical needs.
